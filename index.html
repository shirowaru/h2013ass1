Welcome to Sit On A Rock

Sit On A Rock is an invitation to routinely schedule time to love your soul, honour your humanity, respect your body, understand your brain, connect to nature and through these practices, experience vibrant energy, peace of mind and profound wellbeing.
We bring together ancient practices and integrated body-mind science to offer classes, courses, workshops, coaching, therapies and retreats on we can thrive, even if it is imperfectly, in these challenging circumstances.
Right now, everything is happening online. We hope that we will be free to unlock our doors and welcome you with open arms in person, in 2021!




Wellness


As brain-body occupants, we have a decision to make, today and every day; do we mindlessly live in our sublime bodies, do we handover care entirely to medical professionals, or do we sit on a rock and take responsibility for our own lifestyle, habits and wellbeing?
The team at Sit On a Rock is here to help you decide and begin again, whenever you are ready to journey towards optimal wellbeing.
The once separate arenas of health and wellness have pretty much merged over recent decades and trends indicate that this process will continue.
Research shows that elements of wellness usually include at least: Emotional, Physical, Spiritual, Social, Occupational, Intellectual, Financial and Environmental.
Here at Sit On A Rock our approach to wellness is entirely holistic. We focus on teaching and sharing what we know and what we are passionate about.
Wellness is a $44.2 trillion market and each of us can choose how we invest in our wellbeing.





Yoga


Welcome to YogaTree, Ireland. New to Yoga? There's no need to be nervous - yoga is accessible to everyone, no matter your age, occupation, fitness level or flexibility. Yoga is in no way exclusive and practising yoga has a multitude of benefits. Yoga is a practice that is both physical exercise, helping improve toning, stamina, posture, strength, balance and flexibility, as well as a discipline that helps you de-stress, relax, feel healthier and more energetic.
