Self-Contained Luxury Cottage, Breakfast & Free Yoga


3 Free Live Online Yoga Classes & Continental breakfast included. Airbnb Covid Guidelines for cleaning are strictly observed by the team. Ballycullen Lodge is an exclusive, luxury mountain hideaway on the border of Wicklow Mountains National Park, an ideal getaway; close to Glendalough, Devil’s Glen and Mount Usher Gardens. An hour from Dublin airport and 10 minutes from the motorway. The local landscape has inspired painters, poets and nature lovers for centuries.



The Space

The stone house dates from the 1840s and has been sensitively renovated and extended, featuring all modern comforts. The ceilings are high and the rooms generous. There is one large en-suite bedroom that can be set up as with one king size or 2 single beds. An additional bedroom, with a double bed, that is not en-suite, is available at an extra charge, in particular circumstances, get in touch with the hosts.

Come and experience the local culture, seek out festivals, regattas, sporting events, pubs and restaurants, or simply enjoy the peace and quiet of the countryside. For the gardening or heritage enthusiast, there are botanical gardens, garden exhibitions and historical attractions to explore.

For those wanting to get away from the pressures of daily life and recharge their batteries, come and rest, relax, perhaps read or just be - we will look after you. If you would like an energy boost, our wellness packages include: yoga, forest walks, massage, one-on-one coaching and delicious organic meals in this "wonderful relaxing and elegant retreat, set in stunning gardens in the heart of the countryside”.

Guest Access

The cotage has a separate entrance and includes a cosy guest sitting room with a fireplace, conservatory, kitchen and dining room. An Organic home-cooked breakfast is included. There is ample parking. (The cotage attached to the main lodge)

A FREE 90 minutes yoga class is included with your stay. We also offer WELLNESS PACKAGES (extra cost) including: yoga, forest walks, massage, one-on-one coaching and delicious organic meals. Advance notice is essential. Talk to us about what would make your stay special and we will do our best to arrange it, we like a challenge!

Our focus is healthy living, so we offer vegetarian menus as well as a full Irish breakfast. Special dietary requirements are not a problem for us, provided we have at least 48 hours advance notice.
