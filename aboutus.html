About Us

Hildi Eiselen comes alive when she learns, teaches and shares her passion for self- led wellbeing. She worked as a corporate executive, was CEO of the Trinity Institute (Trinity College Dublin) and practiced as a therapist and leadership coach. She runs Ballycullen Lodge, just south of Dublin, as a retreat centre and takes groups on holistic retreats to Africa. She continues to love teaching yoga weekly, even after 30 years.
She is honoured to share the Sit On A Rock platform with a small, select group of gifted and enthusiastic professionals she sees as her tribe. Specialists in wellness, neuroscience, education, nutrition, mindfulness, yoga, physiotherapy, psychotherapy and so much more!
It’s been hard for us to connect online, as it’s the energy of the students in the room that lifts our souls, but we are learning!


The Team

Hildi works with a group of carefully chosen individuals in a close network of passionate like- minded professionals to offer a wide range of wellness services to individuals and corporate clients.
